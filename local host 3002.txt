  To avoid the warning permanently on a Windows PC, install the cert once:
  1. Copy C:\Users\marom\personal-finance\certs\cert.pem to the other PC (USB, network share, email)
  2. Rename it to cert.crt, double-click it
  3. Click "Install Certificate" → "Local Machine" → "Place all certificates in the following store" → "Trusted Root Certification Authorities" →
   Finish

  After that, Chrome/Edge/Firefox will show the padlock with no warning on that PC.
